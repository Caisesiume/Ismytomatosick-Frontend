Privace Policy

At Tomato Diagnostics, we believe that privacy is a right. We want to empower our users to be the masters of their identity. In this privacy policy, we want to help you understand how and why Tomato Diagnostics ("Tomato Diagnostics,” "we” or "us”) collects and uses information about you when you use our sites, mobile app, widgets, and other online products and services (collectively, the "Services") by us or when you otherwise interact with us or receive a communication from us. We collect minimal information that we use to provide the functionality of the Services. You don't need an account for using the Services. We don't automatically track your precise location. You can share as much or as little about yourself as you want. Any data we do collect is used primarily to provide our services, which are focused on allowing people to recognize serious diseases of tomato plants by providing images of the plants leaves. If you have questions about how we use your data, you can always ask us for more information.


Information You Provide to Us
We collect information you provide to us directly when you use the Services. This includes:

User information
You don't need an account to use Tomato Diagnostics.

When you use Tomato Diagnostics, you may provide other optional information indirectly through your browser or internet connection. This information is not necessary and will not be used by us at any time, with exception to detected malicious activity. We do not store your any preferences nor settings.


Content you submit
We collect the content you submit to the Services. This includes your images uploaded to our service (Images are first uploaded the moment the "UPLOAD" button is clicked).


Other information
You may choose to provide other information directly to us. For example, we may collect information when you fill out a form or request customer support, or otherwise communicate with us.
